About Our Initiative

The Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock Development prioritizes food security, sustainable agricultural practices, farmer support, and innovations in livestock and crop production.

Our goal is to drive economic growth, empower local farmers, and strengthen the nation’s agricultural sector through state-of-the-art machinery and financial support.
